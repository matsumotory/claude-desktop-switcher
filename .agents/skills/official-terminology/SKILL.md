# OFFICIAL TERMINOLOGY RULES

You must ALWAYS use the exact official product names. Do not invent your own localized versions or shorthand unless officially used by the company.

## Anthropic Products
- **Desktop Application (English)**: "Claude Desktop"
- **Desktop Application (Japanese)**: "Claudeデスクトップアプリ" (Do NOT use "Claudeデスクトップ" or "Desktopアプリ")
- **CLI Tool**: "Claude Code" (Do NOT use "Claude CLI" or just "CLI" when referring to the specific product)

## General Rules
1. **Never guess translations**: If you don't know the official Japanese name for an English product, search the web for official PR or documentation.
2. **Consistency**: Use the exact same official term throughout the entire codebase, UI, and documentation.
3. **No Slang**: Do not abbreviate proper nouns in documentation or user-facing text unless the abbreviation is officially endorsed.
