Zero-Impact Isolation

Isolate Claude Desktop Environments.

Customize the separation level of your profiles. This macOS utility unifies the management of both your Claude Desktop App and Claude Code (CLI) environments.

Unified Control of
GUI and CLI

While Claude Desktop and Claude Code are separate tools, this utility manages their backend profiles as one. Switch GUI environments via the menu bar, and sync your external terminal to the exact same isolated environment with a single command.

Customizable Separation

Define exactly what to share and what to isolate. For example, you can completely separate your login sessions and past chat histories between 'Work' and 'Personal' profiles, ensuring your data never crosses over.

Localized Profile Application

Your Mac's global system settings remain untouched. Active profiles are applied strictly locally—only to the Claude Desktop app launched via this tool or your specific terminal tab. This prevents unexpected bleeding into your usual development environment, with the actual isolation level depending on your sharing configuration.

Designed for real workflows.

Adapt the level of isolation to match how you actually work on a single machine.

01

Strict Project / Client Separation

You're managing multiple clients or navigating vastly different technical projects, and you need an absolute guarantee that chat histories and project contexts never intersect.

Selecting "Complete Isolation" assigns physically independent data directories and keychain instances to each profile. Source code and conversations from one client will never structurally leak into another.

02

Dev / Research Sharing

You want separate billing accounts, but wish to share global rules and plugin setups.

Use "Flexible Sharing" to seamlessly sync specific configuration files across profiles while preventing redundant management.

03

Multi-Account Scaling

You need to seamlessly juggle secondary or alternative accounts as your usage grows.

Switch profiles right from the menu bar to instantly access alternative environments.
