Zero-Impact Isolation

Isolate Claude Desktop Environments.

Customize the separation level of your profiles. This macOS utility unifies the management of both your Claude Desktop App and Claude Code (CLI) environments.

Download for macOS
↗
 Read Documentation

Unified Control of
GUI and CLI

While Claude Desktop and Claude Code are separate tools, this utility manages their backend profiles as one. Switch GUI environments via the menu bar, and sync your external terminal to the exact same isolated environment with a single command.

Customizable Separation

Define exactly what to share and what to isolate. For example, you can completely separate your login sessions and past chat histories between 'Work' and 'Personal' profiles, ensuring your data never crosses over.

Localized Profile Application

Your Mac's global system settings remain untouched. Active profiles are applied strictly locally—only to the Claude Desktop app launched via this tool or your specific terminal tab. This prevents unexpected bleeding into your usual development environment, with the actual isolation level depending on your sharing configuration.

Designed for real workflows.

Adapt the level of isolation to match how you actually work on a single machine.

01

Strict Personal / Org Separation

The Need

You must ensure that personal projects and company data never cross-pollinate, preventing accidental chat history leaks.

The Solution

Use "Complete Isolation" to physically separate data directories and configuration files. What happens in one profile stays completely invisible to the other.

02

Dev / Research Sharing

The Need

You want separate billing accounts for development and research, but you want them to share the same plugins and global configurations.

The Solution

Use "Flexible Sharing" to sync specific configuration files across profiles while keeping authentication data strictly isolated.

03

Multi-Account Scaling

The Need

One account simply isn't enough, and you need to seamlessly juggle a second or third account throughout the day.

The Solution

Switch profiles from the menu bar with a single click. Access entirely separate environments without the friction of constantly logging in and out.

Seamless GUI Workflow.

Advanced External Terminal Integration

Leverage your profiles not just in the built-in terminal, but directly within your preferred external terminal apps (like iTerm).

01

Unified Profile Management

Your Desktop app data directories and CLI configuration files are managed together under a single, unified profile.

02

Flexible Sharing

Choose between complete isolation (default) or sharing specific configuration files on a per-profile basis.

03

External Terminal Switching

Simply run eval $(csw env) in your external terminal app to instantly operate Claude Code within the isolated environment of your active profile.
